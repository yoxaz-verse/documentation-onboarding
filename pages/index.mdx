## 🔹 1. Purpose of This Onboarding Portal

- Help you understand **what you need to do as a new member**
- Provide **step-by-step instructions** to get started

## 🔹 2. How This Portal Works

1. **Step-by-Step Process**: Follow the steps in order to complete your onboarding.
2. **Clear Instructions**: Each step explains exactly **what to do, how to do it, and why it matters**.
3. **References to Other Portals**: If you need more information about the system, roles, or commissions, the links above will guide you.
4. **Practical Setup**: Covers everything from **account setup, tools, company rules, and initial tasks**.
5. **Self-Learning Model**:  
   This onboarding follows a **self-learning and real-time experience model** — instead of long training sessions, you’ll learn by actually doing.  
   It’s designed to help you **understand your role, dedication, and work structure** through hands-on activity.  
   Whenever a step requires contacting the team, follow the **specific contact instructions provided in that step** only.
