# 👋 Team-Based Operator Onboarding

You are now entering the **OBAOL team-based operating structure**.

This onboarding is for individuals who have chosen to **operate within a team setup** rather than independently.

> ⚠️ Note:
> This is not a job onboarding.  
> This is a structured setup to help you operate effectively inside real trade execution.

---

## 🔹 1. Purpose of This Onboarding

This onboarding ensures:

- You understand how to operate within a team  
- You follow a structured execution approach  
- You align with how trade coordination actually happens  
- You avoid operating randomly or independently  

This is not training theory.  
This is **operational setup**.

---

## 🔹 2. How This Works

Inside the team setup:

- You will not operate in isolation  
- You will follow a structured approach  
- You will receive guidance during execution  
- You are expected to stay involved  

You are responsible for:

- Following instructions  
- Staying consistent  
- Participating in execution  

---

## 🔹 3. Reference Before Starting

Before proceeding, ensure you have reviewed:

- [Operator Role](https://www.obaol.com/roles/operator)  
- [Commission Structure](https://www.obaol.com/commission-structure)  

These will give you clarity on:
- How you operate  
- How earnings are structured  

---

## 🔹 4. What Changes in Team Mode

Compared to independent operation:

**With Team:**
- You get structured guidance  
- You follow defined processes  
- You work alongside others  
- You learn execution practically  

**Independent Mode:**
- You operate on your own  
- No structured support  
- Full ownership from day one  

---

## 🔹 5. Onboarding Steps

Follow these steps in order:

- **Step 1:** Complete your basic profile  
- **Step 2:** Set up communication channels  
- **Step 3:** Get access to required tools  
- **Step 4:** Start operating within the team structure  

Do not skip steps.  
Execution depends on proper setup.

---

## 🔹 6. Important Clarity

- This is a **performance-based system**  
- There is **no fixed salary**  
- Earnings depend on **completed trades**  
- Your involvement directly impacts outcomes  

This setup exists to:
- Reduce confusion  
- Improve execution  
- Help you operate effectively
