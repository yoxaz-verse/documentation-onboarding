## 🧾 Account Setup & Daily Check-in Guidelines

Welcome to your official onboarding process at **OBAOL Supreme**.

This guide will help you set up your tools and get started with daily tracking:

- ✅ Set up your OBAOL email account via Outlook
- ✅ Join the official Zoho organization
- ✅ Check in and check out every workday for attendance

---

### 📧 1. Outlook Email Login (OBAOL Official Account)

You will receive your official **Outlook email address** and a temporary password from the admin team.

Once received:

- Sign in at [outlook.office.com](https://outlook.office.com)
- Set your new password
- Bookmark the page for easy access

📌 _Only use this email for OBAOL-related communication._

---

### 🏢 2. Join OBAOL Zoho Workspace

After logging in to your Outlook email, you’ll get an invite from **Zoho People** to join our internal organization.

- Accept the invite using your OBAOL email
- Sign in at [people.zoho.com](https://people.zoho.com)
- You’ll now be part of the official team workspace

---

### 🕒 3. Attendance: Daily Check-in & Check-out

We track attendance through **Zoho People**.

Here's what you need to do **every working day**:

- ✅ **Check in** when you start your day
- ✅ **Check out** when your work is done

This is **mandatory**. Skipping it may affect your reports and trust score.

---

### ✅ Summary

- Use only your **official Outlook email** for all internal tools
- Join the Zoho workspace via the invitation link
- Check in & check out **every day** on [Zoho People](https://people.zoho.com)
- Watch the videos above if you’re stuck
- For help, contact your team lead or onboarding guide

📌 _These steps are essential for system access, attendance tracking, and being a verified team member at OBAOL._
