## 🏢 Company Research Specialist (CRS)

**Designation:** CRS  
**Focus:** Company/lead-level validation

---

### 🎯 Role Objective

The **CRS** ensures that every lead passed to the sales team is **complete, validated, and worth pursuing**.

This role is **100% research-focused**:

- **No calling**
- **No selling**
- **No direct prospect engagement**

Instead, CRS is responsible for:

- **Data validation**
- **Lead qualification**
- **CRM accuracy**

---

### 📌 Core Responsibilities

#### **1. Strategic Data Validation**

- Research exporters, suppliers, and key stakeholders in the Indian agro-industry
- Verify **mandatory fields** for every lead:

  - ✅ **Company Name** 🏢
  - ✅ **Verified contact number** 📞
  - ✅ **Verified company address** 📍

- Apply **Five-Point Qualification Criteria**:

  1. General Intent
  2. Specific Intent
  3. Pain Point
  4. Stage (Awareness → Decision)
  5. Role of the Company

- **Only pass** fully validated leads to the **BDA**

---

### 🛠 Standard Operating Workflow (CRS)

1. 📥 **Receive Lead Data** – From trade portals, industry networks, events  
   👉 Submit/receive through this form: [Research Data Form](https://forms.gle/UrgSaXsiR6voX8bj9)  
   📌 _The drive data required for research will be provided. If not received, contact the management team immediately for quick action._

2. 🗑 **Initial Screening** – Remove duplicates, outdated, irrelevant contacts
3. 📌 **Mandatory Field Check** – Verify contact number + address
4. 📊 **Five-Point Qualification** – Assess intent, pain point, stage, role
5. 🏷 **Add Market Tags** – Commodity type, region, buying cycle, urgency
6. 💾 **Update CRM** – Complete fields + qualification notes
7. 📤 **Distribute Intelligence** – Pass validated leads to BDA

---

### ⭐ Golden Rules for CRS

1. **Validate before passing** — never send incomplete or unverified info
2. **Target with precision** — only pass ICP (Ideal Client Profile) leads
3. **Keep CRM clean** — duplicates, errors, and missing data are not acceptable
4. **Think like sales** — every detail helps the team close faster

---

📌 **Key Takeaway:**  
The CRS ensures **every lead is real, validated, and actionable**, strengthening the sales team’s closing ability.
