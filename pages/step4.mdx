# 🏷️ Step 4: Start Working as per Your Designation

Now that your **profile is complete** and you are onboarded in Zoho, your next step is simple:

Start working based on your assigned designation.

---

## 🔹 1. Confirm Your Designation

- Check your assigned role
- Understand your core responsibility
- Focus on practical execution

If you are unsure about your designation, contact your supervisor or the person responsible for onboarding and clarify immediately.

Do not delay work due to uncertainty.

---

## 🔹 2. Join Zoho Cliq (Mandatory)

All coordination and reporting happens inside **Zoho Cliq**.

- Join your assigned groups
- Follow instructions shared by Admin or reporting head
- Stay active and responsive

Cliq is your operational workspace.

---

## 🔹 3. Begin Assigned Tasks

Once your role is clear:

- Start the tasks allocated to your designation
- Follow internal checklists or instructions
- Maintain accuracy and structured reporting

You will learn through real execution, not theory sessions.

---

## 🔹 4. Reporting & Accountability

- Share progress updates in Cliq
- Maintain records of completed tasks
- Clarify doubts immediately
- Prioritize accuracy over speed

---

## ✅ Immediate Action Plan

1. Confirm your designation  
2. Join Cliq  
3. Start assigned tasks  
4. Report progress consistently  

Simple execution.  
Clear communication.  
Professional discipline.
