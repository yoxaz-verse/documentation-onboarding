## 🔚 Final Checklist

- ✅ Use confident, value-oriented language
- ❌ Avoid generic or apologetic phrases
- ✅ Keep messages short (≤5 sentences)
- ✅ Ask meaningful questions
- ✅ End with one strong call-to-action
- ✅ Emphasize trust: _“SSL-encrypted,” “verified leads,” “no scams”_
- ✅ Personalize to crop, region, or recent trade trend
- ✅ Use the expanded table to upgrade weak phrases
- ✅ Practice aloud to replace hesitation with clarity
