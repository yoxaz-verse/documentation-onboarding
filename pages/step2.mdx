# ✉️ Step 2: Create Your Official OBAOL Email

Before joining the Zoho workspace, every team member must **create their official OBAOL email ID**.  
This email will be used for **official communication, workspace access, and system logins**.

---

## 🔹 1. Why This Step Is Important

Having a dedicated company email ensures:

- 🔒 **Secure and verified communication**
- 📁 **Organized data management**
- 🧑‍💼 **Professional identity within the OBAOL team**
- 🔗 **Easy integration with Zoho workspace and internal tools**

> The **sole purpose** of this email is to **receive your Zoho Workspace invitation** and use it for official communication within the company.

---

## 🔹 2. Email Format

Your company email must follow this format:

> **yourname.obaol@outlook.com**

📌 Example:  
If your name is _Ravi Kumar_, your email should be:  
**ravikumar.obaol@outlook.com**

> 💡 Outlook is **highly preferred**, but you may use other email providers if necessary.

---

## 🔹 3. How to Create Your Email

1. Go to [Outlook Signup](https://outlook.live.com/)
2. Click **Create Free Account**
3. Choose your email ID in this format:  
   **yourname.obaol@outlook.com**
4. Set a strong, secure password
5. Complete the signup process
6. Once done, **submit your email ID and password** securely using the form below:

👉 [Submit Email Credentials Form](https://forms.gle/g4JckPHsFHAQBgzE7)

> ⚠️ This submission is **required** for account verification and workspace linking.  
> The credentials will be **securely stored** in our encrypted internal database and used **only** for workspace setup.

---

## 🔹 4. Important Next Step After Submission

After you’ve submitted the form:

> 🚨 **You must immediately inform the company team** (via Whatsapp of company representative shared you this link or your assigned contact) that you’ve completed the submission.

This ensures that the team can take **quick action** to verify your email and send your **Zoho Workspace invitation** without delay.

---

## 🔹 5. Zoho Workspace Invitation

Once your email is verified:

- You will receive an **official invitation from Zoho** to join the OBAOL workspace
- Check your newly created email inbox
- **Accept the invitation** immediately to activate your workspace access
- After acceptance, move to → [Step 3 – Zoho Workspace & Communication](./step3)

---

## 🔹 6. Facing Any Issues?

If you encounter any problems while:

- Creating your email
- Submitting credentials
- Receiving or accepting the Zoho invitation

📞 Contact the **company team** immediately through your assigned communication channel (WhatsApp, or phone).

---

### ✅ Key Takeaways

- Create your company email in the format **yourname.obaol@outlook.com**
- Outlook is **preferred**, but alternatives are allowed if needed
- Submit your **email ID and password** securely using the [official form](https://forms.gle/g4JckPHsFHAQBgzE7)
- **Inform the company team immediately** after submission for faster processing
- The **sole purpose** of this email is to **receive your Zoho Workspace invitation**
- Accept the invite once received and proceed to Step 3

> 💡 Your official OBAOL email is your identity inside the company system — create it carefully, submit it properly, and keep your credentials safe.
