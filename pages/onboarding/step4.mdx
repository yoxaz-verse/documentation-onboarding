# 🏢 Step 4: Zoho Workspace & Start Operating Within the Team

After completing your official email setup and operator registration, the next step is to get onboarded into Zoho and begin operating within the team structure.

---

## 🔹 1. Zoho Cliq Login

You will receive your official OBAOL email ID and password from the company team.

1. Go to [Zoho Cliq](https://cliq.zoho.com/)
2. Sign in using the credentials provided
3. You are now part of the official team workspace

📌 Use your official company email for Zoho and all internal communication.

⚠️ It is your responsibility to ensure that you have received your official email ID and password.

If you have not received them yet, contact the company team.

---

## 🔹 2. Why the Official Email Matters

The official company email is mainly used for:

- Zoho Workspace access
- Team communication
- Attendance and internal coordination

If you also used the same email for OBAOL operator registration, your work setup will usually stay more organized. If not, that is still acceptable.

---

## 🔹 3. Team Communication

Zoho Cliq is the primary communication tool:

- Participate in group chats for announcements and coordination
- Communicate with your BDA, RP, or mentor here
- Use official channels for task updates, queries, and discussions

All work-related communication should happen inside Zoho to maintain transparency and efficiency.

---

## 🔹 4. Start Operating Within the Team

Your setup is now complete.

From here, you move into actual operation as part of the OBAOL team structure.

You are expected to:

- Participate in ongoing trade discussions
- Observe how coordination happens
- Understand how deals move from enquiry to execution
- Stay responsive and avoid unnecessary delays

Learning happens through execution.

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways

- Complete your official email setup before platform registration
- Use the official company email mainly for Zoho and internal communication
- Using that same email for OBAOL registration is recommended for cleaner organization
- Stay active in Zoho and team coordination once onboarding is complete
