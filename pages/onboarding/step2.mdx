# ✉️ Step 2: Create Your Official OBAOL Email

After completing your profile form, the next step is to create your official OBAOL email ID.

This email is mainly for Zoho Workspace access, official communication, and internal coordination.

---

## 🔹 1. Why This Step Is Important

Having a dedicated company email ensures:

- 🔒 Secure and verified communication
- 📁 Organized access to Zoho and internal tools
- 🧑‍💼 A professional identity within the OBAOL team
- 🔗 Cleaner separation between personal email and work-related activity

The primary purpose of this email is to receive your Zoho Workspace invitation and use it for official company communication.

---

## 🔹 2. Email Format

Your company email should follow this format:

`yourname.obaol@outlook.com`

📌 Example:

If your name is Ravi Kumar, your email can be:

`ravikumar.obaol@outlook.com`

💡 Outlook is highly preferred, but you may use other email providers if necessary.

---

## 🔹 3. How to Create Your Email

1. Go to [Outlook Signup](https://outlook.live.com/)
2. Click **Create Free Account**
3. Choose your email ID in this format: `yourname.obaol@outlook.com`
4. Set a strong, secure password
5. Complete the signup process

Once done, submit your email ID and password securely using the form below:

👉 [Submit Email Credentials Form](https://forms.gle/g4JckPHsFHAQBgzE7)

⚠️ This submission is required for account verification and workspace linking.

The credentials will be securely stored in our encrypted internal database and used only for workspace setup.

---

## 🔹 4. What Happens Next

After creating this email:

- Return to the onboarding guide
- Continue to **Step 3 - Operator Platform Registration**
- It is recommended to use this same official email when registering on OBAOL so your platform activity stays organized separately from your personal email

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways

- Create the official company email before platform registration
- Use this email mainly for Zoho Workspace and official communication
- Reusing the same email for OBAOL registration is recommended for organization, but not mandatory
- Submit the email credentials through the official form
