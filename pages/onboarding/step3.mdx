# 🧾 Step 3: Register as an Operator on OBAOL

After creating your official company email, register yourself on the OBAOL operator platform.

This keeps your onboarding sequence organized and helps separate personal email usage from platform/public-facing operator activity.

---

## 🔹 1. Open the Registration Page

Go to:

👉 [https://www.obaol.com/auth/operator/register](https://www.obaol.com/auth/operator/register)

This is the official operator registration page.

---

## 🔹 2. Which Email to Use

It is recommended to use the same official company email you created in Step 2.

Why this is better:

- Your Zoho and OBAOL access stay easier to track
- Personal email stays separate from operator activity
- Team coordination remains cleaner over time

This is recommended for organization, but it is not mandatory.

---

## 🔹 3. Complete the Registration

1. Open the operator registration page
2. Fill in the required details carefully
3. Submit the registration
4. Make sure the account is created successfully

📌 Use real details so your operator account can be recognized correctly later.

---

## 🔹 4. What to Do Next

Once your operator registration is complete:

- Come back to this onboarding guide
- Continue to **Step 4 - Zoho Workspace & Start Operating**
- Use your official company email for Zoho Workspace access and internal communication

---

## ✅ Key Takeaways

- Create the official company email first
- Register on OBAOL after that
- Using the same official company email is recommended for cleaner organization
- Return here after registration and continue to Step 4
