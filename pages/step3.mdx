## 🔹 1. Zoho Cliq Login

1. You will receive your official **OBAOL email ID** and **password** from the company team.
3. Go to [Zoho Cliq](https://cliq.zoho.in)
3. Sign in using the credentials provided.
4. You are now part of the **official team workspace**.

📌 _Use only this OBAOL email for official communication._

> ⚠️ It is **your sole responsibility** to ensure that you have received your official **email ID and password**.  
> If you haven’t received them yet, **contact the company team** — they will provide your login details.

---

## 🔹 3. Attendance Tracking

Attendance is tracked through Zoho. Every team member must:

- ✅ **Check in** at the start of the workday
- ✅ **Check out** at the end of the workday

Skipping attendance may affect your reports and trust score.

---

## 🔹 3. Team Communication

Zoho Cliq is the **primary communication tool**:

- Participate in **group chats** for announcements and team coordination
- Communicate with your **BDA, RP, or mentor** here
- Use official channels for **task updates, queries, and discussions**

> All work-related communication must happen inside Zoho to maintain transparency and efficiency.

## 🔹 4. Zoho Features Summary

| Feature         | Purpose                               |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Workspace Login | Access to official team space         |
| Attendance      | Daily check-in & check-out            |
| Group Chat      | Team communication & coordination     |
| Notifications   | Updates from admin, leads, or mentors |
| Documentation   | Shared templates, scripts, and guides |

### ✅ Key Takeaways

- Login to Zoho Cliq with **official credentials**
- It is **your responsibility** to get your official login credentials from the company team
- Track attendance daily
- Use Zoho for **all official communication and collaboration**
- Follow group chats and notifications for updates
