# 🏷️ Step 3: Follow Your Designation Instructions

Now that your **profile is complete** and you are **onboarded in Zoho**, the next step is to start **performing tasks based on your designated role**.

> Most starters in the **Sales Department** will begin as **CRS (Company Research Specialist)**.

---

## 🔹 1. Visit Designation Portal

- Go to [designation.obaol.com](https://designation.obaol.com)
- Check your **assigned role** and its **responsibilities**
- Focus on **practical CRS tasks first** — don’t worry about commission details yet

---

## 🔹 2. CRS Starter Tasks

- **CRS1 Module:** Go through initial research modules for company data
- **Collect required data:** Contact your **admin team or management** for access to buyer/company information
- **Start research tasks** according to the **CRS checklist**

---

## 🔹 3. Hands-On Real-Time Experience

> Instead of attending multiple theory classes, you will gain **real-time, hands-on experience** working with actual data and live systems.

- This allows you to **learn faster** and understand the **OBAOL system deeply**
- You will experience **how processes work in real scenarios**
- Hands-on learning ensures you **retain knowledge** and perform confidently

---

## 🔹 4. Communication & Reporting

- Use **Zoho Cliq** for reporting your progress
- Ask your **assigned mentor, admin, or RP/BDA** for guidance if unsure
- Keep records of completed research tasks for verification

---

### ✅ Key Takeaways

- Start work **based on your CRS role**
- Focus on **company data collection and verification tasks**
- Gain **hands-on, real-time experience** instead of attending multiple theory classes
- Communicate regularly with **admin or mentor**
- Reference the **designation portal** for role-specific guidance
